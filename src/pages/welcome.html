Flying Lyrics

Ready to sing along? Floating lyrics is here.

Sing along with your favorite tracks while multitasking on your PC. Synchronized, translated, and romanized lyrics floating in a dedicated Picture-in-Picture window.

1

Open Spotify or YouTube Music

Go to your web player dashboard in Spotify or YouTube Music to start playing any music track.

2

Click the FLYING LYRICS Button

Look for the pill-shaped green button injecter in the bottom player bar (Spotify) or top-right (YouTube Music).

3

Rock out in Picture-in-Picture!

A native picture-in-picture window pops up. Keep it on top of other applications while browsing or working.
